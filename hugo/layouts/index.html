About Gloria Deo

Holding fast to the Word of God in a time of relativistic values, the Gloria Deo Classical Homeschool Cooperative seeks to demonstrate the priorities laid forth in Scripture.

Through a full program, committed membership and cooperative teaching, we strive to support each other in homeschooling our children using a classical education philosophy.

Gloria Deo meets one full day a week for 31 weeks on Mondays at Orchard Community Church, 2285 Latta Road in Greece. Gloria Deo Cooperative is a member of Homeschool New York.
